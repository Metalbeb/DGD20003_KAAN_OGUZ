GAME DESIGN DOCUMENT FOR "LANGUAGE BARRIER"

Mechanics:
Language Barrier is a game all about deciphering, unsurprisingly, an unknown language. The player will have to find their way to their classroom in a foregin school 
that they recently got transferred to. By asking around, they will listen to the people speaking to them and will be able to associate unknown words with their own 
interpretations of possible meanings in their notebook. By process of elimination, aided by correct guessing of words, the player will be able to reach their own 
class.

Narrative:
You are Draye Peon, a transfer student at the " @#!? High School ", an anomalous building inbetween realities, not really existing but not really not existing also :P
Most unfortunate for you, you neither speak nor comprehend the language spoken here, ergo you will have to try your best to communicate with the other beings in there
to find your way around.
